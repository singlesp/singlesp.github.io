About

I am a senior research and data scientist at PennLINC where I lead research and development for the SYPRES initiative. I'm also a member of the neuroinformatics team and assist with neuroimaging data curation and software development for our many industry-leading open source tools.

Consulting

I am available for consulting engagements across a range of topics in neuroscience, psychedelics, and drug development. Previous and ongoing projects include:

  • Expert review of books and articles Rigorous scientific review and fact-checking for publishers and journalists
  • New drug candidate evaluation Mechanism-of-action analysis and preclinical-to-clinical translation assessment
  • Psychedelic science advisory Separate fact from hype
  • Clinical trial design Endpoint selection, neuroimaging protocol design, and statistical planning
  • Synthesized evidence reports Systematic reviews, meta-analyses, and living evidence synthesis
  • Neuroimaging data curation BIDS-compliant data organization, QC pipelines, and open data release
  • Software development Full-stack web apps, data dashboards, and analysis tooling
